about

hey! my name is belle cirino and i'm a live audio engineer from portland, oregon. i'm an avid hiker, artist, musician, and nature lover. i started songwriting and producing music for myself in middle school, which quickly turned in to a passion for all things live audio. i thrive in creative spaces, and intentionally place myself in the intersection of creativity and technical skill. more than anything, i'm drawn to people and their passions and goals. i thrive in live music environments not only because of my intense curiosity and love of physics, computers, and music - but also because of my willingness to listen and love of meeting new people!
